First, this website collects news article titles from Google News on a specific DJIA stock. Second, it uses the the Natural Language Tool Kit (NLTK) to tokenizes words included in news article titles collected from Google News. This process separates all words from news articles. Third, the website removes stop words, which are negligible words that do not convey any sentiment, such as 'is' and 'a. Fourth, the website lemmatizes the remaining words, which combines words with the same roots. This ensures that the sentiment analyzer does not process overlapping meanings in the next step. Finally, this website uses the Sentiment Intensity Analyzer from NLTK to assign news titles polarity scores between -1 and 1. That's how this website quantitatively expresses news title sentiments.

News title sentiments are important to technical analysis as they help point out different sentiment contexts for information, which influence technical analysis performances.

The purpose of technical analysis is to interpret current price movements to predict future price movements based on historical market trends.
