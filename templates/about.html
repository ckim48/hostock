Analysis Processes

  1. This website calculated the average polarity score of the last business day’s news title sentiments relating to the stock in question.
  2. This website classified this average polarity score as either ‘positive,’ ‘neutral,’ or ‘negative’ based on the sentiment analysis of past news titles on DJ30 stocks.
  3. This website plugged the average polarity score of the last business day’s news title sentiments into mathematical formulas of 5-day interday income change projection for its five technical indicator algorithms and the benchmark. The formulas were previously obtained by finding logistic regressions of the relationship between average news title polarity scores and trading algorithms’ 5-day interday performances based on historical data and simulations performed using historical data.
  4. Based on the analysis, this website identifies the trading strategy with the brightest prospects and reports the estimated profit/loss margin in percentages. The results only pertain to the specific stock chosen and the date of this website’s use. They may change upon refreshing, as the action may affect news title selection for sentiment analysis.
